Privacy Policy

Bayanihan Express is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address
  • Payment information: Credit card or other payment details
  • Order information: Details about your orders, including products purchased and shipping preferences
  • Tracking information: Information about your location and tracking of your shipments
  • Device information: Information about your device, including IP address, browser type, and operating system

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide our services: Process your orders, track shipments, and provide customer support
  • Improve our services: Analyze your use of our services to improve our products and features
  • Communicate with you: Send you updates, promotions, and other relevant information
  • Protect against fraud: Prevent and detect fraudulent activities

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to:

  • Third-party service providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our business, such as shipping carriers, payment processors, and data analytics companies
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose your information to law enforcement or other authorities as required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information: Request a copy of your personal information that we hold
  • Correct your personal information: Request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information
  • Object to processing: Object to the processing of your personal information for certain purposes
  • Request erasure: Request that we delete your personal information under certain circumstances

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website or by contacting you directly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at: customercare@bayanihan-express.com
